IEM COLOGNE 2025

IEM Cologne 2025 was a major Counter-Strike 2 tournament held from July 23 to August 3, 2025, at the LANXESS Arena in Cologne, Germany. The event had a prize pool of $1,250,000 and featured 24 top teams competing for the title. Team Spirit won the tournament, defeating MOUZ 3-0 in the Grand Final. The tournament included play-ins, a group stage, and playoffs, attracting thousands of fans and offering exciting matches for the CS2 community.
